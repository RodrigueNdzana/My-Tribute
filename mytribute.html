The Father of the Nation

Our Mission

Our mission is to honor the young South African leaders who have faithfully contributed to the struggle against apartheid- Who really fought to create a better future for the next generation; the future that was already abolished if Nelson Mandela had not firmly resolved not to keep the danger eternally.

Our Objectives/ aims

Our main Objectives is to bring forth what we call ‘Harmony’ among South African. Our main focus resole especially amongst young South African as they still carry that anger of apartheid in them. It will be better if we forget about the past and think about the future, and to promote the vision of freedom and equality for all.
